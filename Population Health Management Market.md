<p><strong>Market Expansion Catalysts</strong></p>
<p>Several factors are driving the expansion of the Population Health Management (PHM) market. These include:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Increase in chronic diseases</strong>: With the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, there is a greater need for PHM solutions to manage long-term care.</li>
<li><strong>Technological advancements</strong>: The adoption of AI, big data analytics, and other technologies enhances the ability to track and improve patient outcomes.</li>
<li><strong>Shift toward value-based care</strong>: Healthcare systems are transitioning to models that reward improved patient outcomes rather than the volume of care provided, leading to higher demand for PHM solutions.</li>
</ol>
