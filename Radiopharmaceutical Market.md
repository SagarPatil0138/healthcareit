<h3>Market Definition</h3>
<p>Radiopharmaceuticals are radioactive compounds used in <strong>medical imaging and therapy</strong>. These specialized drugs help in <strong>diagnosing and treating diseases</strong> by delivering targeted radiation to specific organs, tissues, or cells. <strong>Diagnostic nuclear medicine</strong> involves imaging technologies such as <strong>positron emission tomography (PET) and single-photon emission computed tomography (SPECT)</strong>, while <strong>therapeutic radiopharmaceuticals</strong> are used for treating conditions like <strong>cancer and thyroid disorders</strong>.</p>
