<h3>Key Drivers Fueling Market Growth</h3>
<p>The rising global incidence of stroke cases is a primary factor driving the demand for advanced stroke diagnostics. Increasing healthcare awareness, technological advancements in imaging systems, and government initiatives for early stroke detection and management are further fueling market expansion. The growing geriatric population, which is more susceptible to strokes, and the rising adoption of AI-based diagnostic solutions are also contributing to market growth. Additionally, the increasing prevalence of lifestyle-related disorders such as hypertension, diabetes, and obesity is expected to boost the demand for precise stroke diagnostic tools.</p>
