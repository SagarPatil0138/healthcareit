<h2><strong>Market Definition</strong></h2>
<p>The <strong>Custom Procedure Packs Market</strong> refers to the segment of the medical industry that provides pre-packaged sets of surgical instruments, medical disposables, and equipment tailored to specific procedures. These packs are designed to streamline operations, reduce preparation time, minimize contamination risks, and improve surgical efficiency. Hospitals, ambulatory surgical centers, and specialized clinics are key consumers of custom procedure packs, as they enable cost savings and enhance patient care by providing <strong>ready-to-use, standardized solutions for surgical interventions</strong>.</p>
