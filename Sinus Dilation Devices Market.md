<h3>Market Overview</h3>
<p>The <strong>sinus dilation devices market</strong> is experiencing rapid growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic sinusitis, technological advancements in minimally invasive procedures, and growing patient preference for outpatient treatments. Sinus dilation devices are medical instruments designed to open and maintain the sinus passageways, facilitating drainage and reducing inflammation. These devices are widely adopted in hospitals, ENT clinics, and ambulatory surgical centers due to their efficacy in treating chronic rhinosinusitis.</p>
